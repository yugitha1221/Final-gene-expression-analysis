How to Use the App

  • Step 1: Choose Preprocessing or Differential Analysis from the home page.
  • Step 2: In Preprocessing, upload your expression and metadata files, select the preprocessing method, and click Preprocess.
  • Step 3: Download the processed expression data once it’s ready.
  • Step 4: Go to Differential Analysis, upload the preprocessed files, enter the fold change and p-value cutoffs, and click Run Analysis.
  • Step 5: Download the results CSV and volcano plot after analysis is complete.
